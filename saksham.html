We are "Saksham"

Helping today. Helping tommorow

“Saksham- A Helping Hand Foundation” is a Non-Profit body working in Ghaziabad, Delhi NCR and Nearby Areas. The idea for formation of ‘Saksham’ came to fore about ten years ago in 2011 when its founder Mrs. Namita Gaur pitched in by offering small help to the underprivileged children. Saksham is active in all manners in which we can hold a life and humanity together.Saving Our Resources, Poverty, Education, General Awareness, Women Empowerment are the main areas comes in our territorywhich covers around all topics and channels.
